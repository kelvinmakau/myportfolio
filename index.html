ABOUT

I am a Cloud & Technical Support Engineer with a strong foundation in backend development, system troubleshooting, and cloud infrastructure. I’m passionate about using technology to solve real problems, improve user experiences, and build reliable, scalable solutions that make a meaningful impact.

My technical skill set includes Python, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, REST API integration, and hands-on experience working with Linux and Windows environments. I am also AWS Certified (Solutions Architect – Associate & Cloud Practitioner), with growing expertise in cloud architecture, security best practices, and modern infrastructure tools.

Over the past few years, I’ve supported teams and users across diverse environments—troubleshooting systems, maintaining secure networks, integrating applications, and helping organizations adopt cloud-based solutions. I enjoy the challenge of diagnosing issues, improving systems, and ensuring everything runs smoothly end-to-end.

Beyond work, I value balance and curiosity. I enjoy taking quiet walks, reading novels, watching films, swimming, and exploring new ideas. I’m naturally a blend of introverted and extroverted—happy collaborating with others, yet equally comfortable diving deep into focused work.

I am continually learning, especially in the areas of cloud computing, security, and modern application development. I’m always open to connecting with people who are passionate about technology, innovation, and growth. I believe great ideas come from shared knowledge—and I’m excited to keep learning, building, and contributing to meaningful projects in the tech community.
